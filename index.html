About Me

I'm Noam Rotstein, a PhD candidate at the Technion – Israel Institute of Technology, conducting research under the supervision of Professor Ron Kimmel. My work encompasses various aspects of computer vision, including 3D processing, multimodal learning, and image editing using generative diffusion models.

Alongside my academic pursuits, I serve as an AI Research Scientist at Lumana, where I was among the company’s first employees. In this role, I contribute to the development of next-generation, AI-powered video analytics products.
